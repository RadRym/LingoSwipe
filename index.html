⚡ How it works

Three steps. That's it.

1
📱
Open the app
No login, no setup. Pick your language pair and you're in. English, Polish, and French to start.
2
👆
Swipe & tap
Swipe up for the next card. Tap anywhere to flip and see the translation. Repeat as long as you feel like it.
3
🚶
Go back to your life
No lesson to finish, no streak to protect. Close whenever. Language absorbs without forcing.
